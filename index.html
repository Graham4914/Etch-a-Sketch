Etch a Sketch

  • Click and Drag: Draw on the grid.
  • Clear: Erase all drawings and reset the grid to white.
  • Eraser: Toggle this to switch between drawing and erasing.
  • Random Color: Toggle this to draw with random colors.
  • Grid Size: Click to set a new grid size. You can choose between 1 and 100 squares per side.
  • Touchscreen Users: Touch and move your finger to draw on the grid.
